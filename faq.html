Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ClubConnect TCET

What is ClubConnect TCET?

ClubConnect TCET is a centralized platform to explore all student-run clubs at Thakur College of Engineering & Technology. It helps you discover events, join clubs, and stay updated on campus activities.

How can I join a club through this website?

While the site doesn't support direct registration, you can explore club pages and use their provided contact or social links to join or inquire about membership.

Can first-year students participate in club events?

Absolutely! Most clubs actively welcome first-year students. Events, workshops, and even organizing teams often include freshers.

How do I know which club is best for me?

Browse the "Explore" section to view clubs by type — technical, cultural, social, etc. Read their descriptions and recent events to find your fit.

Who built ClubConnect TCET?

This website was developed by students as part of PixxelHack 2025 to make club discovery easier and foster participation in TCET's vibrant community.
